Common questions

Before we connect.

How does onboarding work?
We start with a quick call — 20 to 30 minutes — to get clear on where you are and what you actually need. I'll follow up with a proposal within 48 hours. Most retainer clients are up and running within two weeks. No long intake forms, no unnecessary back-and-forth.
Can I start with one service and add more later?
Absolutely. A lot of clients start with bookkeeping to get their foundation right, then layer in AI reporting once they see how much clearer things get. Bundle pricing applies at any point — if you add a service mid-engagement, you just pay the bundled rate going forward.
Do you work with businesses outside Maryland?
Yes — everything is fully remote. I work with clients wherever you are. We communicate over email, video call, or whatever platform works best for you. Location has never been a constraint.
What if my transaction volume doesn't fit a tier exactly?
The tiers are a starting point, not a rigid box. We'll look at your actual volume during our scoping call and find the number that makes sense. If you're in between, I'd rather price it honestly than shoehorn you into something that doesn't fit.
